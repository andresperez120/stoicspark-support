Privacy Policy for StoicSpark

Last Updated: July 15, 2025

Andres Perez ("we," "us," or "our") operates the StoicSpark mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

Information Collection and Use

StoicSpark is designed to respect your privacy. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect or store any personal information such as your name, email address, or contacts.

In-App Purchases

To provide and manage premium features, we use Apple's in-app purchase framework. All purchase information is handled securely by Apple, and we only receive an anonymous confirmation of your subscription status to unlock features within the app. We do not have access to your Apple ID, payment information, or any other personal data related to your transactions.

Image Saving

The app requires permission to access your photo library for the sole purpose of saving wallpapers to your device. The app does not read, upload, or collect any of your existing photos or videos.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our support page.
